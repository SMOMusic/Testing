How to make Kestrogle your default Search Engine

Coming Soon, involves editing Chrome's files.

  • Open the settings menu. (Click the three dots in the top right and then Settings)
  • Type "Search Engine" into the settings search bar.
  • Click Address bar and search
  • Click Manage Search Engines
  • If Kestrogle is in the list, click the three dots next to it and then press "Make Default". You are now done and can skip the other steps.
  • If you don't see Kestrogle, press Add.
  • Type "Kestrogle" as the search engine, "kestrogle.kestron.repl.co" as the Shortcut, and paste the following into the "URL with %s in place of query" field: "https://kestrogle.kestron.repl.co/search?q=%s".

Look at the URL/Search bar, and press the 3 dots to pull up the menu. Then click "Add Search Engine".
